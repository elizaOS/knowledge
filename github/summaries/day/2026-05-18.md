# ElizaOS Daily Summary: May 18, 2026

Development on May 18 focused on enhancing cross-platform stability and establishing foundational infrastructure for deployment and benchmarking. Key achievements include the introduction of the `elizaos deploy` CLI, improved ARM64/Flatpak support, and the implementation of a side-by-side SWE-bench comparison harness.

## ✅ Completed Work

**Cross-Platform Stability and Packaging**
*   Improved environment compatibility by implementing Windows fallbacks for `plugin-lifeops` and resolving circular symlink issues in the Docker pruner ([elizaos/eliza#7788](https://github.com/elizaos/eliza/pull/7788), [elizaos/eliza#7783](https://github.com/elizaos/eliza/pull/7783)).
*   Expanded CI/CD capabilities by enabling experimental ARM64 support and preparing Flatpak manifests for offline-only build environments ([elizaos/eliza#7787](https://github.com/elizaos/eliza/pull/7787)).

**Deployment and Benchmarking Infrastructure**
*   Launched the foundation for the `elizaos deploy` CLI to streamline monetized app management ([elizaos/eliza#7786](https://github.com/elizaos/eliza/pull/7786)).
*   Integrated a side-by-side comparison harness for SWE-bench Lite to facilitate performance evaluation against opencode ([elizaos/eliza#7785](https://github.com/elizaos/eliza/pull/7785)).
