# Overall Project Summary: May 22, 2026

Development on May 22 focused on hardening mobile and cloud infrastructure, highlighted by the implementation of local voice routing and a transition to a two-tier Hetzner control plane. The team also prioritized system stability through proactive inbox management and the deprecation of legacy container components.

## ✅ Completed Work

**Mobile & Sandbox Infrastructure**
*   Enabled local voice and sandbox routing for mobile platforms to support remote-mode plugins ([elizaos/eliza#7850](https://github.com/elizaos/eliza/pull/7850)).
*   Refined planner and tool contracts to stabilize gpt-oss routing and OpenCode handoffs ([elizaos/eliza#7881](https://github.com/elizaos/eliza/pull/7881)).
