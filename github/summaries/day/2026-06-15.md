**Cloud Infrastructure & Deployment**

*   Activated `APPS_DEPLOY_ENABLED` on the arm path to ensure correct deployment triggering ([elizaos/eliza#8422](https://github.com/elizaos/eliza/pull/8422)).
*   Automated CI/CD for the `headscale` cloud service to ensure consistent environment deployments ([elizaos/eliza#8423](https://github.com/elizaos/eliza/pull/8423)).

**Authentication & Security**
*   Standardized Steward OAuth PKCE across all login surfaces to resolve production login failures ([elizaos/eliza#8350](https://github.com/elizaos/eliza/pull/8350)).
*   Updated the model catalog to use healthy Cerebras defaults, improving gateway stability ([elizaos/eliza#8426](https://github.com/elizaos/eliza/pull/8426)).

**Billing & Operational Integrity**
*   Hardened the container-billing cron job to ensure idempotency and prevent double-debiting ([elizaos/eliza#8273](https://github.com/elizaos/eliza/pull/8273)).
*   Adjusted organization credit balance defaults to $0 to prevent unintended free credit allocation ([elizaos/eliza#8427](https://github.com/elizaos/eliza/pull/8427)).
*   Optimized CI efficiency by implementing label-gated path classifiers for expensive test lanes ([elizaos/eliza#8414](https://github.com/elizaos/eliza/pull/8414)).
