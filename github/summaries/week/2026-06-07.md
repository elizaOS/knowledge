# Overall Project Weekly Summary (Jun 7 - 13, 2026)

## Executive Summary
This week, the ElizaOS team reached a major milestone in the development of Eliza Cloud Apps by transitioning to a modular, automated infrastructure model. These improvements significantly reduce manual operational work and pave the way for a stable, scalable production environment for our users.

### Key Strategic Initiatives & Outcomes

**Building a Scalable and Secure Cloud Foundation**
_Goal: We are creating a robust infrastructure that allows us to host multiple users securely and independently._
- We established a new "data-plane" in [elizaos/eliza](https://github.com/elizaos/eliza) that ensures each user's data and applications remain isolated and secure.
- We automated the setup and management of databases and hosting, removing the need for manual intervention and making the platform more reliable ([elizaos/eliza](https://github.com/elizaos/eliza)).

**Enhancing Agent Autonomy and Ecosystem Growth**
_Goal: We want our AI agents to be more capable and easier to expand through community-driven plugins._
- We enabled agents to manage their own economic tasks by allowing them to delegate skills to sub-agents ([elizaos/eliza](https://github.com/elizaos/eliza)).
- We expanded the framework's capabilities by integrating new third-party plugins, such as tools for social media search and financial data tracking ([elizaos/eliza](https://github.com/elizaos/eliza)).

**Improving Reliability and User Experience**
_Goal: We are focused on fixing stability issues to ensure a smooth experience for developers and end-users._
- We resolved critical login issues, including problems with Google and email-based authentication, to ensure users can access their accounts reliably ([elizaos/eliza](https://github.com/elizaos/eliza)).
- We fixed crashes in our command-line tools to provide a more stable experience for developers building on our framework ([elizaos/eliza](https://github.com/elizaos/eliza)).

## Repository Spotlights

### elizaos/eliza
- **Infrastructure Automation:** Implemented isolated container hosting ([#8293](https://github.com/elizaos/eliza/pull/8293)) and automated database management ([#8304](https://github.com/elizaos/eliza/pull/8304), [#8338](https://github.com/elizaos/eliza/pull/8338)) to support secure multi-tenant hosting.

- **AI Provider Integration:** Stabilized the BitRouter AI gateway ([#8323](https://github.com/elizaos/eliza/pull/8323), [#8332](https://github.com/elizaos/eliza/pull/8332)) and added Atlas Cloud for image generation ([#8380](https://github.com/elizaos/eliza/pull/8380)).
