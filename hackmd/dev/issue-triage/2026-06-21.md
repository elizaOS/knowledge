## Process Improvement Recommendations
1. **Create an “Incident Intake” template** for Discord-reported outages (URL, region, timestamp, console/network logs, status code).
2. **Add DRIs and escalation rules** for every ecosystem product (Cloud, Apps, waifu.fun, partner integrations) with a public “who owns what” page.
3. **Pre-release checklists must be test-backed:** if a feature is behind a flag, require a CI job (smoke + E2E where possible) before enabling in prod.
4. **Publish canonical FAQ entries for sensitive topics** (token strategy, platform relationships, governance) and enforce “link-only” moderation for those topics to reduce speculation loops.
5. **Improve community responsiveness loops:** route partnership inquiries and major product questions into a tracked GitHub Discussion/Issue within 24 hours so they don’t stall in chat.
