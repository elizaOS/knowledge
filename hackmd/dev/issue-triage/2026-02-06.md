## Patterns / Themes Suggesting Deeper Problems
- **Identity + session binding issues across products:** The ElizaCloud email deep-link overwriting behavior and Babylon OAuth/linking failures both point to fragile handling of **state/identity binding** and insufficient guardrails around ‚Äúcreate vs link vs login.‚Äù

- **Operational maturity gap (observability + canaries):** Repeated ‚Äúmerge to production then test‚Äù suggests missing staging parity, canary releases, and automated regression coverage.
