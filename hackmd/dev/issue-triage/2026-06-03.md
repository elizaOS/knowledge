## Patterns / Themes Indicating Deeper Architectural Risk
- **Output integrity is fragile across multi-agent boundaries**: deliverables are correct at the sub-agent layer but are being **lost in envelope stripping, summarization, or retry narration**. This points to missing “structured response carriers” and insufficient separation between **diagnostics/transcripts vs user-facing outputs**.
- **Packaging/bundling boundaries are leaking**: browser surfaces (Vite/Storybook/UI) are being contaminated by Node-only dependencies via transitive imports and export condition mismatches. This is a recurring ecosystem tax that will keep resurfacing until a repo-wide policy is enforced.
- **Recovery/retry signals are entangled with user-visible narration**: if respawn triggers require emitting user-facing error text, filtering becomes dangerous. This suggests the orchestrator needs a clearer internal event channel distinct from chat output.
