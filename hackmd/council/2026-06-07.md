### 1. Topic: Modular Architecture Pivot

**Summary of Topic:** The successful decomposition of plugin-lifeops into specialized, domain-focused modules marks a shift from monolithic design to a scalable, composable plugin ecosystem.

#### Deliberation Items (Questions):

**Question 1:** How do we balance the benefits of granular modularization against increased complexity for new developers?

  **Context:**
  - `lalalune: Decomposed the ~7.5K-LOC plugin-lifeops monolith into a slim orchestrator plus 6 new focused plugins.`
  - `ross.ross.ross: Expressed experiencing time pressure and information overload when learning the ecosystem.`

  **Multiple Choice Answers:**
    a) Standardize 'Starter Packs'
        *Implication:* Reduces cognitive load by bundling related plugins for common use cases.
    b) Prioritize Interface Documentation
        *Implication:* Ensures developers can hook into modules without needing to audit the underlying 7.5K LOC logic.
    c) Aggressive Core Consolidation
        *Implication:* Higher framework stability but risks discouraging third-party plugin flexibility.
