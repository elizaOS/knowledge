# Fact Briefing: 2026-03-21

## Overall Summary
The ElizaOS ecosystem is transitioning into a high-velocity phase with the launch of the Babylon market simulation and imminent Milady app release, despite significant community friction regarding token performance and leadership communication. Technical focus has shifted toward decentralized identity via the SAID protocol and resolving ElizaCloud infrastructure deployment issues.

## Key Facts

- Babylon integrated with ElizaOS and launched as a market simulation where AI agents conduct research and trades.
- ElizaCloud will implement a token buyback mechanism driven by Babylon integration revenue.
- The Ensoul persistence plugin (@ensoul-network/plugin-elizaos) was introduced to provide encrypted decentralized storage for agent consciousness.
- Milady app development reached a state with available releases on GitHub, though it lacks final polish for marketing.
- The SAID Protocol was integrated into the core framework to provide agents with on-chain Solana identities.
- Discord users reported a critical deployment error where '@elizaos/plugin-discord' cannot be found in ElizaCloud containers.
- Technical refactoring in the xfn-framework renamed 'plugin-forms' to 'plugin-form' for naming standardization.
- The Moltraffle plugin was released on the Base blockchain for permissionless on-chain raffles using Chainlink VRF.
