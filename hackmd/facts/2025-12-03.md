### Strategic Insights

#### Multi-user authentication and security infrastructure development
The core development team is prioritizing multi-user authentication with JWK provider support, suggesting preparation for enterprise or SaaS deployments rather than just single-user setups.

*Implications/Questions:*
  - How will this authentication system integrate with the proposed marketplace model?
  - Is this groundwork for the ElizaCloud business model?

#### ElizaCloud marketplace monetization strategy
Discussions point to ElizaCloud adopting a traditional app store model with 30% revenue share from third-party developers, suggesting a focus on building a developer ecosystem rather than just end-user products.

*Implications/Questions:*
  - What incentives will be offered to early developers to build on the platform?
  - How will this model compete with other AI marketplaces?

#### Infrastructure cost optimization
The team is exploring hosting multiple agents on fewer servers instead of isolated serverless instances, indicating a shift in infrastructure strategy possibly due to cost concerns.

*Implications/Questions:*
  - Will this affect the reliability or isolation of individual agents?
  - Is this driven by financial constraints or technical optimization?
