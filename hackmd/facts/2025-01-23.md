### Strategic Insights

#### Telegram distribution risk for non-TON blockchain integrations
Telegram TOS enforcement starting Feb 21 introduces a compliance constraint for Eliza agents operating on Telegram when using third-party blockchains outside TON. This may require product and messaging changes for Telegram-based deployments.

*Implications/Questions:*
