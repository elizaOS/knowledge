### Strategic Insights

#### V2 simplification vs. ecosystem fragmentation
V2 messaging emphasizes simplified setup (`npx elizaos init/start`), but the shift to separate plugin repositories and version divergence (0.1.9 vs 0.25.x vs 1.9) continues to generate onboarding confusion and repetitive support load.

*Implications/Questions:*
  - Should the project publish a single versioned compatibility matrix for clients/plugins across major releases?
