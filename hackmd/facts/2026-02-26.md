# Fact Briefing: 2026-02-26

## Overall Summary
elizaOS is undergoing a major strategic pivot toward immediate revenue generation and profitability, coinciding with critical version control challenges in the main repository and the release of competing frameworks like Nous Research's Hermes Agent.

## Key Facts

- The develop branch was found to contain version 2.0.0 code unexpectedly, leading to the creation of a v2-develop branch to support 1.x users.
- A strategic restructuring focuses on revenue-generating projects including Agent Arena Duel Betting, OTC Agent, and the Babylon trading platform.
- The Hermes Agent framework by Nous Research was released, featuring trajectory compression for fitting training data into token budgets.
- Bidirectional synchronization between GitHub and Linear caused tracking issues necessitating a manual cleanup.
- The organization is prioritizing shipping products like Milady to drive traffic toward Eliza Cloud as a primary revenue source.
- MoonPay launched MoonPay Agents, allowing AI agents to hold wallets and transact in crypto autonomously.
- New infrastructure integrations for WhatsApp, Gmail, and N8N were finalized during the week of February 15-21.
- PR #6510 was opened to integrate the SAID Protocol for on-chain Solana identities for agents.
