# Fact Briefing: 2026-02-19

## Overall Summary
ElizaOS is transitioning into a high-velocity phase for its v2.0.0 and beta launches, focusing on multi-language support (Rust, Python) and decentralized agent identity via the SAID Protocol. Security remains a high priority with successful real-time rugpull detection tests and new trust layer integrations like MoltBridge.

## Key Facts

- Vlt9 reported the successful detection of DARKTRUMP and NVIDIA token rugpulls 2 hours before crashes using a live testing system.
- The SAID Protocol was integrated into the core eliza repository to provide agents with on-chain Solana identity and verifiable signatures.
- PR #6509 initiated 'The Great Database Refactor' to improve system speed by 14x and separate Drizzle dependencies from the core.
- ElizaOS announced a strategic partnership and investment from TSX-V listed firm Secure Blockchain to expand into capital markets.
- The ai16z token migration period officially ended after 90 days, with team members confirming no further support for holders who missed the deadline.
- Plugin-n8n-workflow received a critical bug fix ensuring default property values are applied before visibility checks to stabilize LLM interactions.
- Mobile support improvements were implemented for elizaos.news to enhance community accessibility.
- The team confirmed $elizaos as the main token and established a revenue-based buyback mechanism.
