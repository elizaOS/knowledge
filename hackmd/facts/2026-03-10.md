# Fact Briefing: 2026-03-10

## Overall Summary
The ElizaOS community is currently navigating a period of significant tension characterized by sharp declines in token value and project communication concerns, while simultaneously seeing high-level B2B architectural proposals and expansion into enterprise integrations like WhatsApp and Gmail.

## Key Facts

- Jaime Vejar Aguirre proposed a B2B AI agent architecture for YOYO utilizing LangGraph, MCP, and Supabase with pgvector.
- ElizaOS plugin for ZARQ crypto risk intelligence was published, providing pre-trade scoring for 205 tokens.
- The project confirmed active multi-chain operations currently limited to Solana and BSC (Binance Smart Chain).
- Developers reported persistent model configuration issues across different agents and requested a Google voice plugin to replace ElevenLabs.
- Project leadership confirmed ongoing development of the 'milady project' and the 'Spartan Degen AI' component.
- Documentation and code examples for implementing timed agent-to-agent Discord interactions were provided via the milady-ai and elizaos/examples repositories.
- Integrations for WhatsApp, Gmail, and N8N workflow engine reached final stages of implementation in the core repository.
- The SAID Protocol was introduced to provide agents with verifiable on-chain identities on the Solana blockchain.
