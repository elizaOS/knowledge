# Fact Briefing: 2026-03-26

## Overall Summary
The ElizaOS ecosystem is shifting toward autonomous trading agent development and decentralized identity integrations, punctuated by the launch of the Nosana Builders' Challenge and the release of the Pythia MCP server for on-chain market data.

## Key Facts

- Denis announced a partnership with Nosana for the Builders' Challenge starting March 25, 2026, with technical workshops scheduled for March 26 and April 2.
- Ivan Jeremic released the Pythia MCP server, enabling agents to query Chainlink oracles on Polygon for 13 tokens without API keys.
- A supply chain attack on the 'litellm-pypi' package was identified and acknowledged by maintainer Odilitime.
- The 'The Undesirables' project launched 4,444 NFT-based AI agent workspaces featuring deterministic personality mapping and MemGPT-style memory.
- Developer Naman initiated a search for an AI/ML partner to optimize a 200-500B parameter local LLM for 24/7 autonomous trading.
- The Milady app is confirmed to be in active development with core maintainer Odilitime involved, though no release date is set.
- BitMart's listing team reached out to the ElizaOS core team regarding potential business cooperation.
- The SAID Protocol was integrated into the core framework to provide agents with verifiable digital identities on the Solana blockchain.
