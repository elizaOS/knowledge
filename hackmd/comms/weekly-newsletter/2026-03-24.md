## 3) Community Spotlight (Discord)

### The “Milady timeline” loop—and why it keeps coming up
Multiple community members asked variations of: **“When will Milady be online?”**  
Odilitime’s consistent answer: **no date**, launch **when it’s ready**, and confirmation of **weekend work**.

Takeaway: the community isn’t just asking for hype—they’re asking for *planning signals*. Even a lightweight public checklist (alpha/beta gates, known blockers, what “ready” means) would likely reduce repeated friction.

### Communication expectations vs. what’s being delivered
A recurring tension: members feel updates exist but don’t answer their *core questions* (utility, roadmap certainty, token alignment). Team members pointed to:
- **Weekly video updates** (“cronjob”)
- **Daily updates** in a dedicated channel

Community suggestion that emerged organically: when “no communication” claims appear, respond with a link to the official updates channel so newcomers can orient quickly.

### Ecosystem architecture clarity: ElizaOS vs. Milady vs. OpenClaw
A helpful clarification resolved a key misconception:
- **ElizaOS is the base “operating system for agents.”**
- **Milady is built on top of ElizaOS** (not a replacement).
- **OpenClaw agents can exist within Milady**, meaning these pieces can be complementary rather than competitive.

That architecture framing is important—especially for builders deciding where to invest time and how to think about compatibility.
