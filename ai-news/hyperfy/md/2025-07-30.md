# Hyperfy Discord - 2025-07-30

## Overall Discussion Highlights

### Development & Technical
- **The Forge Platform**: Lastraum mentioned cross-verse chatting functionality coming soon, enabling communication across any metaverse and device.
- **App Architecture**: Discussion about separating functionality into distinct apps (Audio, Zone) versus "uber apps" that combine multiple functions.
- **World Management**: Ashxn explained that zipping up a 'world' folder creates a single file that can serve as a backup or for importing an entire world on any server.
- **Texture Compression Tools**: A tool with visual sliders for texture compression was shared and positively received by community members.

### Project Status & Community Concerns
- **Current Focus**: Pete indicated that Hyperfy is currently in a building phase with "plenty of runway," with marketing planned for later when "all the ducks are in a row."
- **Community Engagement**: Some members expressed concerns about market visibility and price action, with suggestions to increase community engagement through tutorial videos and social media activity.
- **Community Calls**: Confirmed that community calls occur every other week.

### Technical Issues
- **Particle Rendering**: A user reported and self-resolved an issue with particles appearing differently in-world compared to their original design.
- **Internet Connectivity**: Brief discussion about internet speeds and tools for diagnosing local router issues.
