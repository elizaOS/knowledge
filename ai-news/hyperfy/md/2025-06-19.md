### Development Tools & Techniques
- **DeepWiki for Hyperfy Development**: hiroP shared that indexing Hyperfy on DeepWiki is more token-friendly than using a mega-prompt with the entire repo. Ashxn confirmed this works well for making apps, especially for non-coders, though it occasionally hallucinates functions not in the codebase.
