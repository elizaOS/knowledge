# elizaOS Discord - 2025-12-25

## Overall Discussion Highlights

### Token Migration & Price Concerns
- Users report significant concerns about the ElizaOS token's continuous price decline, with many reporting 90-95% losses
- The migration from AI16Z to ElizaOS tokens has been problematic, with technical difficulties and confusion about eligibility
- Only tokens held at the November 11th snapshot can be migrated, and only from the wallet that held them at that time
- The token needs to be mintable to support Chainlink CCIP for cross-chain functionality
- Migration was partly necessary to move away from token2022 which many exchanges won't support
- Significant concern about potential delisting from Korean exchanges (Bithumb, Coinone, and Korbit) in January

### Project Valuation & Market Position
- Discussion about ElizaOS's low market valuation (22M FDV) compared to competitors like Pippin (536M) and Virtuals (693M)
- Community members express frustration about project management, communication issues, and unfulfilled promises
- Some users maintain that the team's intentions are good and suggest continued development could trigger a "short squeeze"
- Odilitime suggests focusing on long-term plans rather than chasing market trends

### Product Development
- Shaw proposed splitting Babylon into points and non-points game components
- Points game could leverage Eliza cloud for inference and authentication, potentially migrating 350,000 users to Eliza cloud
- Plans to create a crypto version of Babylon integrated with Jeju
- Cloud rollout mentioned as a step toward implementing tokenomics
- R0am shared using async agents for maintaining a 40-day streak on tip.md

### Community Sentiment
- The community appears divided between those who have lost faith in the project and those who believe the underlying technology remains viable
- Criticism of the migration's execution and subsequent communication as major factors in the project's current struggles
- Reference to high-profile backers' advice being ignored and long-delayed features

## Key Questions & Answers

**Q: Why does the migration tool show 0 tokens when trying to migrate?**  
A: You can only migrate tokens held at the time of the November 11th snapshot, and only from the wallet that held them at that time (answered by Alexei)

**Q: Why is the token still mintable after migration?**  
A: Chainlink CCIP requires the token to be mintable to enable cross-chain functionality (answered by Odilitime)

**Q: Why did the migration happen in the first place?**  
A: One main technical reason was to move away from token2022 which many exchanges won't support (answered by Odilitime)

**Q: What is the token's use case?**  
A: Cloud revenues drive buybacks (answered by Odilitime)

**Q: Is it too late for the project to recover?**  
A: Rabbidfly suggests recovery is possible if they "keep building" and get "a narrative catalyst"

**Q: How do I remove the cassette animation from my avatar?**  
A: It's a Discord feature, you can edit your profile and remove it (answered by Odilitime)

**Q: How does R0am maintain their tip.md streak?**  
A: "I use async agents and only review when I get home. Then merge and repeat" (answered by R0am | tip.md)
