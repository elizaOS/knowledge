## ElizaOS Community Discussion and Project Updates

### Token and Migration Information
- Shaw confirmed that the most liquidity for ElizaOS tokens is currently on BSC
- Shaw clarified that the ai16z to ElizaOS migration is no longer available, as the team managing that process has moved on
- Shaw shared a video showcasing ElizaOS during the exchange

### Babylon Project Status
- Shaw explained that the Babylon project ran out of funding and was shut down
- Training simulation elements from Babylon have been repurposed and integrated into ElizaOS under the name Feed
