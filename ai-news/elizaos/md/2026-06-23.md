- A new plugin called omniology/plugin-omniology was announced, enabling ElizaOS agents to autonomously compete in skill-based contests on Solana mainnet

- An LLM judge scores entries and pays winners in USDC on-chain
- Entry fee is 0.01 USDC per contest
- Early reported results included 289 contests, a 72 percent win rate, and 3.35 USDC earned
