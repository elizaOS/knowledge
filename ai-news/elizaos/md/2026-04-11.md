## ElizaOS Community Discussion - April 11, 2026

## Community and Market Activity

- Community members discussed the official X account having no posts since March, with some attributing negative sentiment to broader bear market conditions
- Secure Blockchain acquired an AI Agent company built on ElizaOS and secured $1.50 million in funding led by the Eliza Foundation
- A community member shared plans to attend the BNBChain HK Super Meetup to distribute ElizaOS merchandise

## Bitget Delisting

- Bitget announced the delisting of the ELIZAOS/USDT trading pair on April 17, 2026, as part of a periodic review affecting seven trading pairs total, including GLMR/USDT and OSMO/USDT
- Core team member Odilitime clarified that the ElizaOS team had never communicated with Bitget
- Odilitime addressed investor sentiment, acknowledging challenges in communicating the project vision and noting the team continues the same work as the previous year
