## ElizaOS Discord Community Discussion - June 17, 2026

## Open Source Strategy Debate

- Community member Bawss raised concerns about elizaOS remaining fully open source, citing competitive pressures in the AI landscape and suggesting monetization of tools or partial open-sourcing of older versions
- Community members pushed back, with IVgamer noting that being the foundation of a technology retains competitive value regardless of open source status
- IVgamer clarified that a commercialization partnership between Secure Blockchain Development Corp. and the Eliza Foundation already exists for enterprise-level AI agents built on the elizaOS framework
- Ross contributed that providing free access to knowledge represents a form of equity with purpose
- Bawss acknowledged gaps in his understanding and requested further clarification on existing commercialization efforts
