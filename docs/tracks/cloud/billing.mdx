Eliza Cloud bills in **credits**. Credits cover inference, container minutes, storage, and add-ons. They are denominated in fiat (USD) under the hood.

## Top up

Manual top-up via Stripe in the dashboard. Auto-renewal is optional and configurable per account.

## Where credits go

- **Inference** — per-call, per-token cost from the underlying provider plus the app's configured markup.
- **Containers** — per-minute by tier.
- **Storage** — long-term blob, vector, and document storage.
- **Add-ons** — domains, premium features.

## Creator monetization

Apps with monetization enabled earn a share of inference markup and any purchases. Earnings accrue per `appId` and are payable through Stripe Connect (or other configured rails).

## Invoices and receipts

Issued automatically on every top-up and at billing cycle close. Available in the dashboard and via the Cloud API.
